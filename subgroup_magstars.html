Magnetically Variable Stars Subgroup

Topic Area

The Magnetically Variable Stars subgroup studies the photometric variability from stars induced by the presence of surface magnetism. This includes Sun-like stars whose brightness is modulated by the passage of dark active regions (starspots) and bright faculae, as well as RS Cvn and T Tauri variables.
