This is a list of current TVS members who have reached "builder" status. A TVS SC â€œbuilder" is defined as TVS members who contributed significantly through their survey for at least one year to the TVS SC. This includes serving in any of the boards (membership, publication, builders-status board, or policy-review board) or as subgroup coordinator, or as lsst-stack support team, or as spokesperson or in any other active role in a TVS SC task force, as defined by the task force itself. The builder status is achieved by a process of nomination and award, with the final decision resting in the hands of the builder-status board. Simply sitting on a board or any of he bodies specified above does not guarantee the achievement of builder-status. Builders have the right to be asked if they want to be co-authors on TVS publications, as defines in this document. The builders should not abuse this right, and evaluate if their contribution to TVS has in fact facilitated the publication. Their co-authorship on a paper can be appealed and the publication board is the body who has the right to deny a builder co-authorship on a TVS publication.
