SN Ibc

Listen to a type Ibc Supernova (SN Ibc or Stripped Envelope SN). These stars are stripped of their outer envelopes before explosion. The SN brightening lasts a few months.

SN Ia

Listen to a type Ia Supernova (SNIa). These supernovae are standard candles, which means they can be used to measure the expansion of the Universe. The SN brightening typically lasts about one year, and they tend to be bluer than most SN Ibc.

Eclipsing Binary

Listen to an Eclipsing Binary: a pair of stars that orbit each other and trade places along our line of sight.
